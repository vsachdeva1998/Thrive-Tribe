List of Agreed Time and Fees

Date: June 26th, 2024

This List of Agreed Time and Fees dated as of June 26th, 2024 (the “Effective Date”), is entered into by and between Jenkins Dentistry for Kids (“Customer”) and CE Computech LLC (“CEC”), pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth in the Master Services Agreement (“MSA”), dated as of June 26th, 2024, between the parties.

1. Services Offered

CEC will be providing service of Dental revenue Cycle Management back-office work to the Client (the “Services”).

2. CEC Responsibilities

Within the Services to be provided, CEC will provide the following:

  • List of updated protocols to perform assigned task based on updates received from Client.
  • Accomplish the above within a mutually agreed upon time frame.
  • Communicate with Client on a regular basis and provide mutually agreed upon reports detailing CEC activity.
  • Make all reasonable efforts to ensure a minimum 97% overall accuracy on the total volumes per Month.
  • Make team available to work 5 days a week, Monday to Friday excluding national holidays.

3. Client Responsibilities

Client shall provide CEC with:

  • List of protocols to be followed to perform assigned task.
  • Access to practice management system to enter appropriate details.
  • A list of policies and procedures utilized by Client.
  • Specific software and procedural training as may be required to perform its obligations under this Exhibit.
  • Updates through training and documentation on operating manuals as software or procedures change.
